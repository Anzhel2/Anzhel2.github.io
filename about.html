About Me

Hello! I'm Anzhela Safaryan, a passionate reels maker dedicated to crafting engaging content that resonates with audiences. My journey began at university, where I was crafting content for University's Student Council. At some point, I realized that I would like to make content that sticks to people's minds for a long time even outside of University.

I love bringing ideas to life through visual storytelling, helping brands connect with their viewers in meaningful ways. My work focuses on creating high-quality reels that capture attention and drive engagement.

Studying Contemporary Communication at LCC International University, I understood the power that creativity holds. It is very crucial to be able to transform the vision into action through video elements. Creative minds lead into successful ideas and my core is to be able to help you to turn those ideas into visually pleasing reality.

My approach is to stick to the branding, but at the same time blend modern and trendy elements.
